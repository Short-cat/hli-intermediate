Artificial Turf Installation

We install high-quality synthetic turf for lawns, pet runs, play areas, and low-maintenance landscapes. Each install includes a properly compacted base, drainage layer, and secure seam work. The result is a clean, green space that looks great year-round without watering or mowing.

  • Installed over compacted base with drainage system
  • Ideal for lawns, play zones, side yards, and pet areas
  • Available in various turf heights, colors, and textures

Custom Putting Green Installation

We design and install custom putting greens for residential backyards or recreational spaces. Greens are shaped for realistic performance and paired with fringe turf for chipping and short game practice. Cup placement and layout are fully tailored to your space.

  • Includes turf selection, cups, contours, and fringe
  • Designed for realistic roll, bounce, and slope
  • Built to fit any yard shape, size, or skill level

Base Preparation & Grading

A strong foundation is essential for any putting green or turf area. We excavate and install a compacted base with proper slope and drainage systems to ensure long-term stability. This prevents pooling, uneven spots, and shifting over time.

  • Graded and compacted aggregate base
  • Ensures smooth, even playing surface and long-term durability
  • Includes drainage layer to prevent water buildup

Putting Green Contouring & Shaping

We shape putting greens with subtle breaks, undulations, and slopes to create realistic short-game practice. These features are custom-built during base prep for long-lasting performance. Greens can include multiple breaks, drop-offs, or practice zones.

  • Adds slopes, dips, and breaks to challenge your game
  • Mimics real putting conditions and green movement
  • Customized during installation to fit your yard and style

Fringe Turf & Landscape Integration

Fringe turf blends the green into the rest of the yard while creating a buffer for chipping and approach shots. We also integrate plant beds, paver walkways, or boulders around the area for a clean, finished look. The transition from putting green to landscape is seamless.

  • Includes turf fringe around putting green perimeter
  • Enhances both aesthetics and short-game practice
  • Can be paired with landscaping, plant beds, or hardscapes

Infill & Surface Finish Options

Infill is applied to synthetic turf to help blades stand upright and improve performance. We offer a variety of infill types like silica sand, rubber, or specialty blends. Each is selected based on how you use the turf—whether for play, pets, or putting.

  • Adds structure, weight, and performance to turf
  • Available in sand, rubber, or pet-safe options
  • Improves traction, ball roll, and turf longevity

Turf Repair & Replacement

If your existing turf is worn, damaged, or outdated, we offer partial repair or full replacement. We assess the condition, reset base layers if needed, and match or upgrade the turf type. This service restores both the look and function of your space.

  • Repair or replace worn, stained, or lifted turf sections
  • Option to upgrade to modern, higher-performance turf
  • Seamless blending with existing turf and landscaping
