Our Fence Installation Process

Initial Consultation and Property Assessment

We begin with an on-site evaluation to assess your property’s needs, discuss fencing preferences, and recommend the best fence type for your space.

System Design and Cost Estimate

Based on our assessment, we provide a detailed quote that includes the fencing cost, labor, and necessary materials. We ensure clear and upfront pricing to help you make an informed decision.

Site Preparation and Planning

Before installation, we clear the designated area, remove any old fencing, and prepare the ground to ensure a stable foundation for your new fence.

Fence Installation and Finishing Touches

Our team expertly installs each section of your fence, ensuring tight seams and structural integrity. We then add finishing details like fence gates and post caps for a polished look.

Final Walkthrough and Care Instructions

Once the installation is complete, we provide maintenance tips to ensure the longevity of your new fence. We also conduct a final walkthrough to address any questions and confirm your satisfaction.

How Much Does Fence Installation Cost in Houston, TX?

General price ranges for fence installation in Houston

  • Small Fence Installation (up to 50 linear ft.): $1,200 - $3,500
  • Medium Fence Installation (50 - 200 linear ft.): $3,500 - $8,000
  • Large Fence Installation (200+ linear ft.): $8,000+

Several factors affect fencing cost in Houston

  • Fence Material: Wood fencing, vinyl fencing, and metal fencing vary in pricing.
  • Property Size: Larger areas require more materials and labor.
  • Fence Design: Custom fences and privacy fences may have higher costs.
  • Accessibility: Difficult terrain or obstacles can impact installation efficiency.
