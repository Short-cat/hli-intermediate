About HLI

Houston’s Landscaping Partner—Yesterday, Today, and Tomorrow

Time is a valuable thing—and time is what has made Houston Landscape Images the dependable landscaping company it is today. With over 30 years of dedication, and 1,000+ projects, we have grown strong in the Houston community.

As a family-run business, we bring that experience to every job—landscaping, grading, irrigation, tree services, and our specialty in hardscaping—all with one skilled team. No need for multiple contractors; we streamline the process to maximize your investment. Whether it’s a large-scale installation or a custom home design, we deliver results that exceed expectations. We’re not just here for a single project—we aim to be your trusted team for every landscaping need, year after year.
