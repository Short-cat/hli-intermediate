Our Complete Lawn Care Services List

Professional Grass Cutting Service

Our precise mowing services follow the one-third rule, never removing more than a third of the grass blade length to promote healthy growth and prevent stress. We adjust cutting heights seasonally and use well-maintained equipment to ensure clean cuts that prevent disease and maintain your lawn's aesthetic appeal.

Lawn Edging Service

We create crisp, clean lines along sidewalks, driveways, and landscape beds that give your property a manicured, professional appearance. Our detailed edging service includes both horizontal and vertical cutting to maintain distinct boundaries between your lawn and hardscaping elements.

Lawn Fertilization Service

Our fertilization program provides your lawn with essential nutrients at crucial growth stages. We use slow-release fertilizers custom-blended for Houston's soil conditions and your specific grass type, whether it's St. Augustine, Bermuda, or Zoysia.

Lawn Weed Control

We implement both pre-emergent and post-emergent weed control strategies to combat common Houston weeds. Our treatments target problematic species while protecting your desired turfgrass, ensuring a weed-free lawn throughout the growing season.

Aeration and Overseeding

Combat soil compaction and improve grass density with our professional aeration services. We follow up with overseeding when necessary to fill in thin areas and enhance your lawn's overall appearance and health.

Lawn Fungicide Treatments

Houston's humidity can create perfect conditions for fungal diseases. Our preventive and curative fungicide applications protect your lawn from common problems like brown patch and take-all root rot.

Seasonal Yard Cleanup

We provide thorough cleanup services throughout the year, removing leaves, debris, and thatch that can suffocate your lawn and create breeding grounds for pests and diseases.

Special Seasonal Considerations for Houston Lawns

Houston's unique climate requires specialized seasonal care approaches:

Spring Lawn Care (March-May)

  • Pre-emergent weed control application
  • Initial fertilization for growth season
  • Mowing height adjustments for warming temperatures
  • Pest monitoring as activity increases

Summer Lawn Care (June-August)

  • Adjusted watering schedules for heat stress prevention
  • Regular mowing to maintain optimal grass height
  • Disease monitoring during humid periods
  • Targeted pest control as needed

Fall Lawn Care (September-November)

  • Post-summer recovery treatments
  • Preparation for winter dormancy
  • Fall fertilization program
  • Leaf removal services

Winter Lawn Care (December-February)

  • Winter weed control
  • Reduced mowing frequency
  • Brown patch prevention
  • Frost protection when needed
