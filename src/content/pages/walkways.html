Pathway & Walkway Installation

We build new pathways and walkways using durable materials like concrete, pavers, flagstone, or gravel. Each installation is designed for stability, proper drainage, and seamless integration with the surrounding landscape. Layouts can be straight, curved, or custom-shaped based on your space.

  • Installed on a compacted base with proper slope and drainage solutions
  • Material options include concrete, pavers, natural stone, or gravel
  • Custom layouts to match your property’s flow and function

Decorative Finishes & Patterns

Decorative finishes can turn a basic walkway into a standout design feature. We offer stamped concrete overlay, colored stains, and custom scoring to create texture, contrast, and visual interest. These finishes enhance curb appeal while maintaining durability.

  • Stamped, stained, or textured finishes available
  • Mimics the look of stone, tile, or brick
  • Wide range of patterns, textures, and color options

Garden & Landscape Pathways

These pathways are designed to connect plant beds, garden zones, or backyard features. We build soft, natural paths using gravel, stepping stones, or flagstone that blend into the landscape. Every path is designed for comfort, beauty, and easy upkeep.

  • Built with gravel, stepping stones, or flagstone
  • Connects plant beds, patios, sheds, or outdoor kitchens
  • Designed for a natural look and easy maintenance

Side Yard & Utility Walkways

Side yard walkways provide essential access to gates, utility areas, trash bins, and HVAC units. We build low-maintenance paths that stay level and safe year-round. Materials are selected for durability and drainage in narrow or shaded spaces.

  • Durable surfaces that hold up to foot traffic and weather
  • Ensures clean, dry access to side yards or service areas
  • Options for concrete, pavers, or compacted gravel

Walkway Repair & Resurfacing

Damaged, uneven, or aging walkways can be repaired or resurfaced for safety and appearance. We offer solutions like patching, leveling, or applying concrete overlays. This helps extend the life of your paths without a full rebuild.

  • Repairs cracks, uneven surfaces, or worn finishes
  • Improves safety and updates the appearance
  • Resurfacing options available for concrete or paver paths

Walkway & Path Lighting

We install low-voltage lighting along walkways and paths to improve safety and visibility after dark. Lights are placed to gently illuminate edges, steps, and curves without overwhelming the space. Fixtures are weather-rated and integrated into the landscape for a clean, finished look.

  • Includes path lights, recessed step lights, or bollard-style fixtures
  • Enhances nighttime safety, navigation, and curb appeal
  • Available in warm or cool tones, with timers or smart control options

Accessibility & Grading Adjustments

We adjust walkway slopes, widen paths, or install ramps to improve accessibility and drainage. These modifications are ideal for wheelchairs, strollers, or mobility needs. All work is done to ensure safe, smooth movement across your property.

  • Slope correction, widening, and ramp installation
  • Improves mobility and eliminates trip hazards
  • Designed to meet ADA standards where needed
