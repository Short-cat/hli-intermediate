Our Walkway Installation Process

  1. Initial Consultation and Lawn Assessment

    We assess your site, discuss material options, and create a plan for your stone walkway, paver walkway, or concrete sidewalk.

  2. Excavation and Foundation Preparation

    We clear the designated area, level the ground, and install a stable foundation to prevent shifting or settling over time.

  3. Walkway Installation

    Our skilled walkway installer team precisely lays walkway pavers, ensuring proper alignment, spacing, and secure placement.

  4. Finishing Touches and Edging

    We install walkway edging, seal pavers (if applicable), and add finishing details like decorative gravel, landscaping, or lighting.

  5. Final Walkthrough and Cleanup

    We ensure the site is clean, your new walkway meets expectations, and provide care instructions for longevity.

Several factors affect paver installation costs

  • Material Selection: Natural stone, concrete, and brick vary in price.
  • Walkway Size & Complexity: Larger paths and intricate patterns require more labor.
  • Site Conditions: Excavation, grading, and drainage needs can impact costs.
  • Additional Features: Walkway edging, lighting, and landscaping can add to the overall investment.
