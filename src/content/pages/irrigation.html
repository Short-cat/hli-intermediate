Our Irrigation and Sprinkler System Installation Process

Initial Consultation and Site Assessment

We begin with an on-site evaluation to assess your landscape’s watering needs, discuss your preferences, and determine the most suitable irrigation system for your property. We also address any concerns regarding installation and maintenance.

System Design and Cost Estimate

Based on our assessment, we provide a detailed quote that includes the irrigation system installation cost, labor, and necessary materials. We offer clear and upfront pricing to help you understand the investment based on the size of your landscape and the type of system selected.

Site Preparation and Installation

Before installation, we prepare the site by addressing any existing issues, such as removing old systems or repairing underground utilities. We then install the irrigation components, including pipes, sprinkler heads, controllers, and sensors, ensuring optimal placement for efficient water distribution.

System Testing and Calibration

Our team carefully tests the entire irrigation system to ensure all components are functioning correctly. We calibrate the system to provide even coverage, prevent water wastage, and meet your specific watering requirements.

Post-Installation Training and Walkthrough

Once the installation is complete, we provide instructions on how to operate and maintain your new irrigation system. We also perform a final walkthrough to address any questions and ensure you are fully satisfied with your new watering solution.

Types of Irrigation and Sprinkler Systems We Install in Houston, TX

Drip Irrigation Systems

Ideal for gardens, flower beds, and areas requiring precise watering.

Rotary Sprinklers

Perfect for large lawns and open landscapes needing wide coverage.

Fixed Spray Sprinklers

Suitable for smaller lawns and compact areas requiring consistent watering.

Smart Irrigation Controllers

Automated systems that adjust watering schedules based on weather conditions.

Rain Sensors

Devices that prevent the irrigation system from operating during rainfall, conserving water.

Soaker Hoses

Flexible hoses that provide slow, deep watering for garden beds and shrubs.

Bubbler Irrigation Systems

Deliver water directly to the base of plants, reducing evaporation and runoff.
