We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

We will not use your information for purposes unrelated to these activities without your consent.

We currently do not use cookies or tracking technologies on our Website. If implemented in the future, we may use:

  • Essential cookies (for website functionality)

We will notify users of such changes and provide options to manage or disable cookies via a consent tool, in addition to your browser settings.

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure. Data collected through our Website is stored securely via Netlify and is accessible only to authorized personnel for the purposes outlined in this policy.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy (e.g., to provide services or meet legal obligations), typically no longer than 5 years after your last interaction with us, unless otherwise required by law. To exercise these rights, contact us at houstonlandscapeimages@gmail.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days. You may also withdraw consent for communications (e.g., unsubscribe from emails) by following the instructions in our messages or contacting us directly
