Our Landscape Lighting Installation Process

How Much Does Landscape Lighting Cost in Houston, TX?

General price ranges for landscape lighting installation in Houston

  • Small Landscape Lighting Installation (up to 5 fixtures): $500 - $1,500
  • Medium Landscape Lighting Installation (5 - 15 fixtures): $1,500 - $5,000
  • Large Landscape Lighting Installation (15+ fixtures): $5,000+

Several factors affect landscape lighting cost in Houston

  • Lighting Type: LED, solar, or low voltage lighting options have different price points.
  • Installation Complexity: Wiring and fixture placements can influence costs.
  • Property Size: Larger landscapes require more fixtures and wiring.
  • Fixture Quality: Premium and custom lighting designs may increase the overall cost.
