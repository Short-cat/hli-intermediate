Our Concrete Overlay Installation Process

  1. Initial Consultation and Surface Assessment

    We begin with an on-site evaluation to assess your concrete surface, discuss overlay preferences, and recommend the best concrete overlay option for your space.

  2. Material Selection and Cost Estimate

    Based on our assessment, we provide a detailed quote that includes the concrete overlay cost, labor, and required surface preparation. We ensure transparent pricing to help you make an informed decision.

  3. Surface Preparation and Repairs

    Before installation, we clean and repair the existing surface to ensure a strong foundation for the new concrete overlay. This step prevents future cracks and imperfections.

  4. Concrete Overlay Application and Finishing

    Our team expertly applies the overlay, ensuring smooth, even coverage and long-lasting durability. We then apply any requested decorative finishes, such as stamped concrete overlay textures.

  5. Final Walkthrough and Care Instructions

    Once the installation is complete, we provide maintenance tips to ensure the longevity of your new surface. We also conduct a final walkthrough to address any questions and confirm your satisfaction.
