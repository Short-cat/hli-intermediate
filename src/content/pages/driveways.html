Our Driveway Installation Process

  1. Consultation and Site Assessment

    We begin with an on-site evaluation to determine your driveway needs, recommend materials, and discuss design preferences.

  2. Material Selection and Cost Estimate

    Based on our assessment, we provide a detailed quote that includes material costs, labor, and any necessary site preparation. Our transparent pricing ensures you understand the investment involved.

  3. Site Preparation and Grading

    Proper groundwork is essential for a durable driveway. We clear the area, level the foundation, and install necessary reinforcements to support long-term performance.

  4. Driveway Installation and Finishing

    Our team installs the driveway using high-quality materials, ensuring smooth application and a long-lasting surface. For decorative driveways, we apply stamping, texturing, or sealing finishes.

  5. Final Walkthrough and Maintenance Recommendations

    After installation, we provide care instructions to help maintain the driveway’s appearance and durability. We also perform a final walkthrough to ensure the work meets your expectations.

How Much Does Driveway Installation Cost in Houston, TX?

General price ranges for driveway installation in Houston

  • Small Driveway (up to 200 sq. ft.): $2,500 - $5,000
  • Medium Driveway (200 - 1,000 sq. ft.): $5,000 - $12,000
  • Large Driveway (1,000+ sq. ft.): $12,000+

Several factors affect driveway installation costs

  • Material Choice: Concrete, asphalt, and decorative options vary in cost.
  • Site Preparation Needs: Grading and excavation impact total expenses.
  • Driveway Size: Larger installations require more materials and labor.
  • Design Complexity: Custom decorative driveways may require additional work.
