Tree Trimming & Pruning

We provide expert tree trimming and pruning to improve health, shape, and structure. Our team removes dead, low-hanging, or crowded branches to promote healthy growth and reduce risk. Services are tailored to each tree type and surrounding conditions.

  • Includes crown thinning, shaping, and deadwood removal
  • Promotes airflow, sun exposure, and long-term tree health
  • Improves safety and overall appearance

Tree Removal Services

A well-executed removal starts with a planned approach. We deliver safe tree removal tailored to Houston’s codes and conditions.

  • Full range of services: cutting, stump grinding, debris cleanup, and hauling
  • Compliance with city codes ensures safety, clearance, and stability
  • Purposeful methods boost functionality, safety, and appeal

Tree Removal

We remove trees that are dead, diseased, damaged, or located too close to structures. Removals are done safely using controlled methods and proper equipment. Cleanup and hauling are included to leave your property clear and ready for next steps.

  • Full removal of trees, from canopy to ground level
  • Ideal for trees causing safety, space, or property issues
  • Includes debris removal and optional stump grinding

Emergency Tree Services

Storm-damaged or fallen trees can be dangerous and disruptive. We offer fast-response emergency tree removal and limb clearing to restore safety and access. Priority is given to trees impacting homes, driveways, or power lines.

  • Rapid removal of hazardous limbs or fallen trees
  • Helps clear access and prevent further property damage
  • Available after storms, high winds, or structural failure

Stump Grinding & Removal

After tree removal, we grind or remove stumps to clear space and prevent regrowth. Our equipment reaches below ground level to eliminate root flare and allow for replanting or hardscaping.

  • Grinds stumps below grade for full removal
  • Prevents tripping hazards and future tree sprouting
  • Clears area for sod installation, mulch, or new plantings

Canopy Raising & Thinning

Canopy trimming lifts low branches and selectively thins tree crowns for improved light, clearance, and air flow. This service is ideal for trees near walkways, driveways, or rooftops.

  • Raises canopy height for clearance and aesthetics
  • Reduces weight and wind resistance in tree crowns
  • Improves sunlight for turf, garden beds, or patios

Lot & Land Clearing

We remove trees, brush, and overgrowth to prepare land for construction, landscaping, or resale. Clearing is done efficiently and safely, with grading and debris removal available as needed.

  • Full clearing of trees, brush, and small vegetation
  • Ideal for development, landscape renovation, or property prep
  • Includes hauling and optional grading

Tree Health Assessment

We evaluate tree condition to determine whether trimming, treatment, or removal is needed. Our assessments consider disease, root damage, and structural integrity. This helps you make informed decisions about tree care and safety.

  • On-site inspection of tree health and structure
  • Identifies pests, disease, damage, or stress
  • Provides recommendations for trimming or removal
