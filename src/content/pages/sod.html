Our Sod Installation Process

Initial Consultation and Lawn Assessment

We begin with an on-site evaluation to assess your lawn's condition, discuss your grass preferences, and determine the most suitable sod type for your property. We also address any concerns regarding installation and maintenance.

Sod Selection and Cost Estimate

Based on our assessment, we provide a detailed quote that includes the sod installation cost, labor, and necessary soil treatments. We provide clear and upfront pricing to help you determine the cost of sod based on the size of your lawn and the type of sod selected.

Site Preparation and Grading

Before installation, we remove old grass, debris, and weeds. We also grade the land to ensure even water distribution and add necessary soil amendments for enhanced sod growth.

Sod Installation and Rolling

Our team carefully lays each sod roll to ensure tight seams and a smooth finish. We then roll the sod to improve soil contact and enhance root establishment.

Post-Installation Care and Final Walkthrough

Once the installation is complete, we provide watering and care instructions to ensure healthy sod growth. We also perform a final walkthrough to address any questions and make sure you are fully satisfied with your new lawn.

  • Accessibility: Difficult terrain or obstacles can impact installation efficiency.
