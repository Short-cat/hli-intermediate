Sod Installation

We provide full-service sod installation for new or renovated lawns. Each project includes site prep, delivery, and expert installation of fresh-cut sod. We lay sod in staggered seams and press it in for instant curb appeal and proper root contact.

  • Installed with tight seams and proper base preparation
  • Provides an instant, green, finished lawn
  • Available for front, back, or full-yard applications

Lawn Removal & Site Prep

Before installing new sod, we remove old grass, weeds, and debris to ensure a clean base. We also grade the soil to improve drainage and prevent low spots. This prep work ensures the new sod establishes evenly and quickly.

  • Removal of old grass, roots, and debris
  • Grading for smoothness and proper water flow
  • Prepares soil for healthy root development

Soil Conditioning & Amendment

Healthy soil is the key to a lasting lawn. We amend your soil with compost, sand, or topsoil to improve texture, nutrients, and water retention. This step promotes faster rooting and long-term turf health.

  • Adds organic matter or sand to improve soil structure
  • Boosts drainage and nutrient availability
  • Tailored to match sod type and local soil conditions

Sod Selection & Sourcing

We help you choose the right type of sod for your yard based on sunlight, foot traffic, and maintenance needs. We source fresh-cut sod from trusted local suppliers to ensure quality and performance.

  • St. Augustine, Bermuda, Zoysia, and other varieties available
  • Matches climate, usage, and care preferences
  • Fresh, local sod delivered and installed promptly

Edging & Lawn Shaping

We define clean, curved or straight edges where sod meets plant beds, driveways, or walkways. This gives the lawn a crisp, professional look and helps prevent grass from spreading into unwanted areas.

  • Clean transitions between lawn, beds, and hardscape
  • Helps maintain lawn shape and minimize overgrowth
  • Options for steel, stone, or trench-style edging

Watering & Establishment Guidance

After installation, we provide a detailed watering schedule to help the sod root successfully. We guide you on how to avoid overwatering, footprints, or dry patches during establishment.

  • Custom watering plan based on season and sod type
  • Tips for early care, mowing, and fertilizing
  • Helps reduce stress and promote strong root growth

Sod Repair & Patching

We repair damaged or thinning areas of lawn without a full replacement. This includes cutting out bad sections, prepping the soil, and installing fresh sod to match.

  • Ideal for pet spots, traffic wear, or drainage issues
  • Matches existing grass for a seamless blend
  • Quick and cost-effective solution to revive lawns

Lawn Lighting

We install low-voltage landscape lighting to accent your lawn and improve nighttime visibility. Lights can be placed along edges, around trees, or within planting beds to highlight your landscape.

  • Includes path lights, uplights, or in-ground fixtures
  • Enhances curb appeal and lawn visibility after dark
  • Available with timers, dimmers, or smart controls
