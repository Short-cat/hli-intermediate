How Much Does Plant Bed Installation Cost in Houston, TX?

General price ranges for plant bed installation in Houston

  • Small Plant Bed Installation (up to 50 sq. ft.): $300-$650
  • Medium Plant Bed Installation (50-150 sq. ft.): $650-$1,200
  • Large Plant Bed Installation (150+ sq. ft.): $1,200-$3,000+

Several variables affect plant bed installation cost in Houston

  • Bed Style: Types like raised garden beds or cinder block raised garden beds can vary in price.
  • Location: Accessibility and proximity to structures affect installation complexity.
  • Soil Condition: Poor soil may require amendments or additional labor.
  • Materials: Edging for flower beds, mulch, and soil treatments can add to total costs.
