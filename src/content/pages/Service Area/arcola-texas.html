Landscape Design for Arcola Properties

We install plant beds to define and elevate your outdoor space. Beds are prepped with amended soil and fitted with irrigation lines, using plants like lantana or salvia suited for local conditions. The result is a low-maintenance appeal that holds up year-round.

Artificial Turf Installation for Arcola Yards

Artificial turf offers a practical alternative to grass in high-traffic areas. We lay commercial-grade turf over a compacted base, ensuring no shifting or water pooling—ideal for pets or play zones. It’s built to handle Houston’s climate without fading.

Patio Construction in Arcola

We construct patios as functional extensions of your home. Using concrete pavers, concrete pour, or natural stone, we size them—often 300 square feet or more—to fit your needs, with proper sealing for durability. It’s a solid base for outdoor living.

Patio Covers for Arcola Homes

Outdoor Kitchens for Arcola Backyards
