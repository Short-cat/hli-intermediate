Our Fire Pit and Water Feature Installation Process

  1. Initial Consultation and Site Assessment

    We start by visiting your property to inspect the designated installation area. During this assessment, we evaluate space constraints, safety considerations, and aesthetic goals. We also discuss fuel options, including wood-burning, propane, and gas fire pit solutions.

  2. Design Plan and Material Selection

    Next, we develop a detailed landscape design incorporating your chosen fire pit and water feature. We help select the best materials, such as natural stone, metal, or concrete, and ensure compliance with local codes and regulations.

  3. Site Preparation and Installation

    Before work begins, we prepare the area by clearing debris and ensuring proper foundation setup. Our team installs all necessary gas lines, drainage systems, and structural supports, ensuring long-term durability and safety.

  4. Fire Pit and Water Feature Installation

    We execute the installation according to the finalized design plan. Whether installing a backyard fire pit or an elaborate outdoor water fountain, our skilled craftsmen ensure seamless integration with your landscape.

  5. Final Walkthrough and Maintenance Recommendations

    After installation, we conduct a thorough walkthrough to ensure everything meets your expectations. We provide maintenance tips and offer ongoing service options to keep your outdoor features in excellent condition.
