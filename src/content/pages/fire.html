HLI’s Fire & Water Feature Options

Fire Feature Installation

We design and build custom fire features including fire pits, bowls, fireplaces, and tables. Each installation is tailored to the space and can be built with stone, stucco, or concrete overlay finishes. These features add warmth, ambiance, and a natural gathering point to your outdoor space.

  • Includes gas or wood-burning fire pits, bowls, and fireplaces
  • Built with durable materials like stone, brick, or concrete
  • Can be round, square, linear, or custom-shaped

Water Feature Installation

We install decorative water features such as fountains, waterfalls, streams, and bubbling rocks to add motion and sound to your landscape. Water features can be standalone or integrated with patios, gardens, or pools. Every system is designed for aesthetics, ease of use, and low maintenance.

  • Options include wall fountains, rock waterfalls, and spillways
  • Adds movement, sound, and tranquility to outdoor spaces
  • Installed with pumps, reservoirs, and filtration systems

Custom Design & Integration

We incorporate fire and water features into your overall landscape design for balance and cohesion. Each feature is placed with attention to flow, safety, and visual impact. Our team works to make every element feel intentional and well-connected.

  • Designed to complement patios, seating areas, or garden zones
  • Blended into stonework, walls, or outdoor kitchen layouts
  • Ensures proper spacing, scale, and visual alignment

Gas Line & Electrical Hookups

We handle the utility work required to power fire and water features, including gas lines for burners and electrical lines for pumps and lighting. All hookups are installed safely and to code. This allows your features to operate efficiently and reliably.

  • Underground utility lines installed and concealed
  • Supports fire ignition systems, pumps, and lighting
  • Installed to meet local safety codes and requirements

Fire & Water Feature Repair

If your feature isn’t working properly, we offer repair services for burners, pumps, ignitions, or finishes. We diagnose the issue, source compatible parts, and restore full function. Repairs can also include resurfacing or re-sealing older units.

  • Service for gas burners, ignition systems, and water pumps
  • Repairs cracks, leaks, or worn stone finishes
  • Replacement of outdated components when needed

Lighting & Surround Finishes

We install outdoor lighting and finish materials around your fire or water feature to complete the look and improve nighttime visibility. This includes accent lighting, seat walls, stone borders, or decorative caps. These elements elevate the feature and integrate it with the landscape.

  • Includes path lights, uplights, or integrated LED strips
  • Optional seating walls, coping, or tile accents
  • Enhances safety, visibility, and overall design cohesion
