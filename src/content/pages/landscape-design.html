What is Landscape Design? An Overview of Front Yard and Backyard Re-Design

Professional landscape design involves the systematic planning and arrangement of outdoor elements to maximize property functionality and aesthetic appeal. Our designers evaluate your property's specific characteristics, including soil composition, drainage patterns, and sun exposure, to develop practical solutions. The design process integrates your preferences with technical requirements to create outdoor spaces that meet both immediate needs and long-term objectives.

Our Landscape Design Process

Design Consultation

We begin with a comprehensive site evaluation and client meeting to document your requirements, assess existing conditions, and discuss budget parameters. This phase includes detailed measurements, soil testing, and analysis of existing vegetation and structures.

Custom Landscape Design

Our design team develops detailed construction documents including scaled drawings, plant specifications, and material selections. We provide clear visual representations of the proposed design through professional CAD drawings and detailed plant lists.

Installation

Our installation teams follow industry-standard procedures to implement your design, maintaining strict quality control measures throughout the construction process. We coordinate with utilities, obtain necessary permits, and ensure compliance with local building codes.

Final Walkthrough

We conduct a systematic inspection of all installed elements to verify proper implementation. This includes testing irrigation systems, verifying plant placement, and ensuring all hardscape elements meet specifications.

Ongoing Support

We provide detailed maintenance documentation and scheduled follow-up visits to monitor plant establishment and system performance. Our support includes seasonal maintenance recommendations and prompt response to any concerns.

Landscape Design Services We Offer

Custom Landscape Planning

With years of experience in landscape design, we tailor each plan to match your property’s terrain, soil type, and climate. We detail exactly where hardscapes, plants, and utilities should go to ensure everything fits together seamlessly. Our step-by-step documentation covers material choices, installation methods, and upkeep recommendations to help you enjoy your space for years to come.

Plant Selection and Layout

Drawing on our in-depth knowledge of growth patterns and water needs, we handpick plants that thrive in local conditions. We focus on native and climate-adapted species to keep your maintenance tasks and water usage low. By placing each plant to maximize sunlight and airflow, we create healthy, beautiful landscapes that stay vibrant all year.

Hardscape Design

Our expert team designs patios, walkways, and other hardscape elements that withstand the elements and daily use. We carefully choose materials—like stone, pavers, or concrete—for durability and a cohesive look. To prevent water buildup and erosion, we factor in proper drainage from the start, ensuring a stable, long-lasting outdoor environment.

Softscape Design

We use proven techniques for soil preparation and planting to help your landscape flourish naturally. By choosing the right plant varieties and arranging them thoughtfully, we create balanced, visually appealing outdoor spaces. Our designs stand the test of time, requiring minimal adjustments while maintaining a lush appearance season after season.

Outdoor Living Space Design

With a focus on comfort and usability, we design patios, seating areas, and outdoor kitchens that fit your lifestyle. We account for traffic flow, safety features, and easy access to utilities so you can relax or entertain without hassle. Every space is completed with lighting, drainage, and high-quality materials for a seamless extension of your home.

Garden Landscape Design

Our garden designs blend aesthetic appeal with practical upkeep, whether you want a tranquil flower garden or a lively, pollinator-friendly spot. We specify soil amendments, irrigation setups, and plant spacing to encourage vigorous growth. By understanding local soil conditions and climate, we ensure your garden stays healthy and inviting year-round.

Outdoor Lighting Design

We enhance safety and highlight the best features of your landscape using energy-efficient lighting. Our team pinpoints the ideal places to install fixtures, whether it’s along pathways or around unique architectural elements. Each lighting plan follows electrical standards and complements the overall look of your outdoor space, making it welcoming at any hour.

Water Feature Design

From tranquil fountains to flowing waterfalls, we design water features that suit your style and space. Our expertise ensures proper filtration and reliable flow systems, so you can enjoy clear water without extra hassle. We also provide detailed installation instructions and maintenance tips to keep everything running smoothly.

Erosion Control and Drainage Design

We safeguard your property by creating grading and drainage solutions tailored to the local environment. This includes features like swales or French drains to channel water away from vulnerable areas. By focusing on proper water management, we help preserve your outdoor space’s stability and value.

Irrigation System Design

Our irrigation designs prioritize water efficiency and plant health, drawing on the latest technology to reduce waste. We break your landscape into specific zones so each area receives the right amount of water. By using tools like drip systems and smart controllers, we help keep your plants hydrated while keeping utility costs under control.
