About Us

Orellana Construction & Remodeling

Orellana Construction and Remodeling has been serving the Greater Houston Area as a leading Construction Company since our inception in 2001. However, we have been working construction literally all of our lives. Because of this vast experience, we know how daunting building and remodeling can be and are here to make the process smooth and easy for you.

We’re not only family but together we comprise a team of experienced professionals who tackle everything from complex projects to simpler operations - no job is too big or too small. What ever your next construction project may be, Orellana Construction is your top choice.
