Our Steps

HLI’s Landscaping Process

  • 01

    Initial Consultation

    During the initial consultation, we will discuss your needs, goals, and budget. We evaluate the property's layout, existing elements, and environmental conditions to develop a tailored landscape design.

  • 02

    Site Analysis & Design Development

    We conduct an in-depth site analysis, assessing soil conditions, drainage, and sunlight exposure, then use these insights to create a detailed plan blending appeal, utility, and local compliance (e.g., HOA rules). This ensures your design is both functional and stunning.

  • 03

    Budgeting and Cost Estimation

    Before moving forward, we provide a clear, itemized cost breakdown to ensure the project fits your budget without compromising on quality.

  • 04

    Installation and Final Walkthrough

    Once installation is complete, we conduct a thorough final walkthrough. We provide maintenance guidance and answer any questions, ensuring you feel confident in the long-term care of your landscape.
